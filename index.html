Tour Packages

We offer best customised tour & holiday packages to explore your destinations.

Ooty Tour Package

Nestled in the lush Nilgiri Hills, Ooty, the "Queen of Hill Stations," offers stunning tea gardens, serene lakes, and majestic mountains. Enjoy a boat ride on Ooty Lake, stroll through the Botanical Gardens, or admire views from Doddabetta Peak. Ideal for nature lovers and those seeking relaxation, Ooty is the perfect getaway.

Munnar Tour Package

Nestled in the Western Ghats, Munnar is a breathtaking hill station famed for its rolling tea plantations, misty mountains, and pristine valleys. Explore the Eravikulam National Park, visit the Tea Museum, or take in the views from Top Station. Perfect for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers, Munnar is an enchanting retreat.

Alleppey Tour Package

Known as the "Venice of the East," Alappuzha is a captivating blend of tranquil backwaters, lush paddy fields, and serene canals. This charming town offers houseboat cruises, picturesque views, and vibrant festivals. Ideal for those seeking a unique and relaxing escape, Alappuzha promises a memorable experience.

Valparai Tour Package

Perched in the Anamalai Hills, Valparai is a serene haven with lush tea gardens, dense forests, and abundant wildlife. Discover the beauty of the Sholayar Dam, trek through the verdant hills, or enjoy the tranquility of the Aliyar Dam. Valparai is ideal for those looking to connect with nature and experience untouched beauty.
